Projects

We love to engage in a variety of partnerships that span multiple disciplines. We are constantly thinking about how to solve new challenges for our partners — here are some of the current projects the HUE team is working on:


hrsimple

We are collaborating with hrsimple to improve their website's overall user experience and increase accessibility to online publications related to human-resource laws and policies.

Chef Farid

We conducted the in-depth user/consumer marketing research to understand cooking service and launched an initial landing page for Chef Farid's cooking service.

Fabric SocialAR

We collaborate with Fabric Social AR for the UX discovery research about how we can strengthen Gen Z’s social fabric with peer-to-peer engagement and connection with social belonging.

Lexan Learning

Lexan Learning is a tutoring company offering specialized remote tutoring services catered to student needs. They offer assessments at every grade level that assess student proficiency & use research based methods to help foster academic excellence.

AIMR Analytics

We conducted user research for financial intelligence webapp to understand target user's characteristics and attitude toward the financial advisory intermediate service.
