Projects

We love to engage in a variety of partnerships that span multiple disciplines. We are constantly thinking about how to solve new challenges for our partners — here are some of the current/past projects the HUE team has been working on:


Healing & Creativity

We conducted user reserach to design a community based experience for a better emotional-wellbeing with everyday creativity.

Music Learning

We are planning a market/user research for music learning with artificial intelligence.

hrsimple

We collaborated with hrsimple to improve their website's overall user experience and increase accessibility to online publications related to human-resource laws and policies.

Chef Farid

We conducted the in-depth user/consumer marketing research to understand cooking service and launched an initial landing page for Chef Farid's cooking service.

Fabric SocialAR

We collaborated with Fabric Social AR for the UX discovery research about how we can strengthen Gen Z’s social fabric with peer-to-peer engagement and connection with social belonging.

Lexan Learning

Lexan Learning is a tutoring company offering specialized remote tutoring services catered to student needs. They offer assessments at every grade level that assess student proficiency & use research based methods to help foster academic excellence.

AIMR Analytics

We conducted user research for financial intelligence webapp to understand target user's characteristics and attitude toward the financial advisory intermediate service.

Car Restoration

We design user experience for car restoration webapp to provide informative joy and customer connection.

La Historia Historical Society Museum

We collaborate with the LA history museum for content management design and web accessibility.
